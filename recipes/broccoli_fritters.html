Broccoli Fritters

Description

A handful of ingredients commonly found in most kitchens is enough to create this simple appetizer!

Ingridients

  • 10 ounces chopped broccoli
  • 2/3 cup flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 ounces shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 1 teaspoon Mexican seasoning mix (such as Goya® Sazonador Total)
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons sour cream, or to taste
  • Casero Pollo Asado Seasoning (Lawry's®) to taste

Steps to make

  1. Place chopped broccoli in a microwave-safe bowl. Pour 3 tablespoons water over the top and cover with a plate. Microwave on high for 2 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a non-stick skillet over medium-high.
  3. Use a cookie scoop to portion out about 2 tablespoons of mixture. Drop broccoli mixture into hot oil and flatten out with a spatula. Cook broccoli fritters until golden brown, about 3 minutes per side.
  4. Transfer cooked fritters on a cooling rack set over a baking tray. Serve with sour cream and casero pollo asado seasoning.
